Our Story

The inspiration for our Political Action Committee, comes from a desire to address the inaction and the overt support for policies contrary to the interests and values of the Muslim community, particularly regarding the crisis in Gaza.

We reject the notion that we must support the lesser of two evils.

We will no longer stand by the party that courts our vote, but uses our tax dollars to subsidize genocide.

The influence of certain lobbies over our politicians was clear that it’s not about what’s right, but about who is funding their campaigns.

So we are taking action to counter that influence.

We are a group of change-makers funded by our community. This PAC was built and influenced by Muslims from all walks of life.

We are Elected Officials

We sit on elected seats at the local, state and federal level. We work as staff for our elected officials.
